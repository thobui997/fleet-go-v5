<objective>
## Goal
Create the shared `FormSection` component and apply it — with focus fix and 2-column grid layouts — to all 5 form dialogs that remain as dialogs after Phase 10.

## Purpose
Currently all form dialogs use a single-column vertical stack inside `overflow-y-auto`, which (a) clips focus rings on edge inputs and (b) forces unnecessary scrolling on medium-complexity forms. This plan fixes both problems without touching the 4 forms that will move to full pages in plans 10-02 through 10-05.

## Output
- `src/shared/ui/form-section.tsx` — new shared component
- `src/shared/ui/index.ts` — exports `FormSection`
- 5 updated form dialogs: Vehicles (2-col + sections + focus fix), Customers (2-col + sections + focus fix), Stations (sections + focus fix), Vehicle Types (sections + focus fix), Roles (focus fix only)
</objective>
