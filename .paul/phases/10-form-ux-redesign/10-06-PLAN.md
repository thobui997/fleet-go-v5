Migrate booking creation form from dialog (BookingCreateDialog) to a dedicated full-page layout following the established Phase 10 pattern.

- New `BookingFormPage` component with full-page layout, FormSection grouping, useBlocker
- Updated routes, router, and list page
- Deleted `booking-create-dialog.tsx`
